Installation FAQs

Installation time varies depending on the size of the space, the number and type of acoustic treatments, and site conditions. A typical home studio might take a few hours to a day, while larger commercial spaces could take longer. We provide a time estimate after the design phase.

We generally ask that the areas where treatments will be installed are clear of obstructions. Specific preparation details will be discussed and confirmed with you during the pre-installation review.

Our experienced installers are equipped to handle various wall and ceiling types, including concrete, drywall, plaster, and wood. We use appropriate hardware and techniques for each material to ensure a secure and lasting installation.

While we primarily install treatments sourced through our design process to ensure quality and compatibility, we may be able to install client-provided treatments on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us to discuss your specific situation.
