About Us

Our Story

Evolve Acoustics was founded in 2023 by Avi Marmer with a singular passion: to transform spaces through the power of sound. We believe that every environment deserves acoustic excellence, whether it's a recording studio, a conference room, or a home theater.

Creating an ambience and elevating experiences through sound is at the heart of everything we do. Our team's combined experience in acoustic engineering, music production, and sound physics allows us to deliver truly custom and perfect acoustic treatments, getting it right the first time.

Our Approach

We combine cutting-edge acoustic science with practical, customized solutions. Each project begins with careful analysis, moves through detailed design, and concludes with precise implementation. The result? Spaces that sound as good as they look.

Meet Our Team
