Your Path to Perfect Acoustics: Our Proven Process

We take a methodical approach to each consulting project:

  • Analyze: On-site acoustic measurement and analysis
  • Identify: Pinpointing problem frequencies and acoustic issues
  • Model: Computer modeling of acoustic behavior
  • Design: Custom solution design based on space requirements
  • Select: Material selection guidance
  • Implement: Implementation oversight and final testing
