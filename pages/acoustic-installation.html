The Evolve Installation Standard

At Evolve Acoustics, installation is more than just hanging panels. It's the critical final step where meticulous planning and design are transformed into tangible acoustic excellence. Our approach is built on:

Precision & Accuracy

Every panel, diffuser, and trap is installed according to exact design specifications for optimal acoustic performance.

Safety & Durability

We use appropriate mounting hardware and techniques to ensure all treatments are secure, safe, and built to last.

Cleanliness & Respect

Our team works efficiently, maintains a clean workspace, and respects your property throughout the installation process.

Our Meticulous Installation Process

We follow a structured approach to ensure every installation is a success, delivering both acoustic performance and aesthetic integration:

1

Pre-Installation Review

Confirming design plans, site readiness, and coordinating logistics with you for a smooth start.

2

Site Preparation & Layout

Careful marking of placement locations based on acoustic design, ensuring precision before any fixtures are made.

3

Professional Installation

Utilizing specialized tools and techniques for secure and visually appealing mounting of all acoustic elements.

4

System Integration (if applicable)

Ensuring acoustic treatments work harmoniously with existing or new audio/visual systems.

5

Final Inspection & Cleanup

A thorough walkthrough to ensure your complete satisfaction and a spotless environment post-installation.
