Acoustic Performance: Is There Really a Difference?

Let's address the most critical factor first: how these fabrics affect sound. Both Guilford of Maine (particularly their FR701 line) and Camira's Synergy/Blazer ranges are specifically engineered to be acoustically transparent, meaning they allow sound waves to pass through with minimal resistance.
